## About GitWriter

GitWriter is a minimal, writer-first mobile Markdown editor backed by Git. We believe in:

- **Simplicity** - Clean, distraction-free writing
- **Ownership** - Your data lives in your GitHub repositories
- **Privacy** - We don't store your content on our servers
- **Open Source** - Transparent development and community contributions
